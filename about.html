About Me: My Beginnings with Brazil

My interest in Brazil started through music.

The first time I heard Samba, a Brazilian music genre, was in the French film, Samba . The song "Take it Easy My Brother Charles" by Jorge Ben Jor was played in the movie. Once I heard Samba I started to explore other genres of Brazilian music such as Bossa Nova , Musica Populara Brasileria (MPB), Pagode, and others forms of music. From that moment on, I developed an interest in not only Brazilian music, but the culture too.
