Oi, tudo bom?

I'm Fatima Coulibaly, the founder of Beijos Brasil.

My passion for learning about Brazilian culture motivated me to share my knowledge with other people. The purpose of this website is to help teach people who aren't familiar with Brazilian culture about it. I would love to engage in conversations and embark in new connections with you all. If you want to know more about Carnaval or learn some Portuguese you came to the right place!

Visit my About Me page to learn about my early interests in Brazil.

Goals I want to Help You Accomplish:

  • To gain a better understanding of Brazilian culture
  • To learn basic Portuguese (Br) vocabulary
  • To be open-minded
  • To motivate you to study abroad

Let the adventure begin!
